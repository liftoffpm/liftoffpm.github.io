What is LiftoffPM?

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide students with interview coaching that is direct and practical, sharing the same lessons that have landed 90+ of our students in their dream PM roles at FAANG companies and top startups.

Free Lessons

We offer free product management interview lessons on our YouTube channel, as well as career advice from PMs and other leaders across the industry.

Paid Course

The LiftoffPM paid course includes additional must-know content. We've taken great care to curate only the most important information so that you don't need to sift through the thousands of hours of content on other platforms or courses.

Results + Care

Not only do we get our clients results, we also love and find joy in maintaining relationships with them over time. Please do not hesitate to reach out with questions or just to say hi!

Paid Course FAQ

Who is this course made for?

If you are feeling lost navigating the endless resources at your disposal or are feeling you've studied so much but can't quite breakthrough to "Strong Hire"-level interviews, then this course is for you. We created this course to be suited for audiences ranging from early to later career PMs. If you're an APM hopeful, a mid-career ladder transfer hopeful, or a seasonsed product manager, then this course is for you. The strategies and tips we provide have had proven results at all levels of seniority; we have helped students land their dream roles from APM all the way to VP of Product.

How many hours of materials are included?

We have intentionally kept the course to 1.5 hours of instructional content. A common pain point we hear from students is information overload. We've worked hard to present the most important information you need to know, rather than making you sift through hundreds of hours of content on other platforms or courses.

Is there any sort of community as part of this paid course?

Not right now, for this reason: when you're learning, you don't want the "blind leading the blind". You want expert advice. Another common pain point we hear from students is that when you do peer mock interviews, you're not sure if the feedback you're getting is legit or not, because they are likely in a similar situation as you. BUT, there are many communities to join if you are still interested. Where relevant, we recommend Lenny's List and Women in Product.

What do the course lessons cover?

The LiftoffPM paid course follows a very similar format to the free lessons on YouTube, so if you're finding value from the free channel, we feel confident you will find value in the paid course. The course covers topics in: Improving a Product, Building a Product, Growing a Product, Creating a 5 Year Roadmap, Root Cause Analysis, Estimation and Sizing, Advanced Behavioral, Take Home Assignments, and How to Navigate Your Interviews with Confidence.
